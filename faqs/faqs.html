Frequently Asked Questions

Sizing & Fit

Our sizing charts are designed specifically for Indian ethnic wear. Each garment includes detailed measurements in both inches and centimeters. We recommend:

  • Taking measurements in the morning
  • Using a fabric measuring tape
  • Following our measurement guide video

Sustainability

Our commitment to sustainability includes:

  • Using organic and naturally dyed fabrics
  • Supporting traditional handloom weavers
  • Minimizing waste through efficient cutting
  • Using recycled packaging materials

Artisan Support

Yes! We work directly with women artisans across India, ensuring:

  • Fair wages and safe working conditions
  • Skill development programs
  • Direct profit sharing
  • Preservation of traditional crafts

Shipping & Returns

We offer a 30-day return policy for international orders:

  • Items must be unworn with original tags
  • Shipping costs for returns are buyer's responsibility
  • Refunds are processed within 7-10 business days
  • Custom orders are non-returnable

Tracking your order is simple:

  • Login to your account and visit 'Order History'
  • Click on your order number
  • Use the tracking number provided in your shipping confirmation email
  • Contact customer service for additional assistance
