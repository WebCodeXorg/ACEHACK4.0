How to Measure

Bust

Measure around the fullest part of your bust, keeping the measuring tape parallel to the ground.

Waist

Measure around your natural waistline, at the narrowest part of your waist.

Hip

Measure around the fullest part of your hips, typically 8" below your waist.

Length

For kurtas and dresses: Measure from shoulder point to desired length. For pants: Measure from waist to ankle.
