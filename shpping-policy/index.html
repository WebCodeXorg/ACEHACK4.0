Shipping Policy

Delivery Timeline

We process and ship all orders within 2-3 business days. The estimated delivery time is:

  • Metro Cities: 3-5 business days
  • Other Cities: 5-7 business days
  • Remote Areas: 7-10 business days

Shipping Charges

We offer the following shipping options:

  • Free shipping on all orders above ₹999
  • Standard shipping fee of ₹99 for orders below ₹999
  • Express delivery available at additional cost

International Shipping

Currently, we only ship within India. We are working on expanding our shipping services to international locations.

Shipping Restrictions

We currently do not ship to:

  • PO boxes
  • Army/Navy bases
  • Certain remote locations
