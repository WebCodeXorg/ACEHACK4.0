Return Policy

Return Period

We accept returns within 7 days of delivery. The product must be unused, unworn, and in its original packaging with all tags attached.

Return Process

Step 1: Initiate Return

Contact our customer service team through email or phone to initiate your return request.

Step 2: Package Item

Pack the item securely in its original packaging with all tags and labels intact.

Step 3: Return Shipping

Ship the item back using our provided return shipping label or your preferred courier.

Refund Policy

  • Refunds will be processed within 5-7 business days after receiving the returned item
  • The refund will be credited to the original payment method used for purchase
  • Shipping charges are non-refundable unless the return is due to our error

Non-Returnable Items

  • Intimates and personal care items
  • Items marked as "Final Sale"
  • Custom-made or altered items
  • Items without original tags and packaging

Need Help?

Our customer service team is here to help you with any questions about returns.
